Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use CoFinance:

  • Email address and password for account creation
  • Budget data including income sources and expenses
  • Activity logs of changes made to your budget
  • Device information for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide budget management services
  • Sync data across your devices
  • Allow collaboration with budget partners
  • Maintain app functionality and security

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase
  • All data transmission is encrypted
  • We implement industry-standard security measures
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel

Data Retention and Cleanup

  • Activity logs are automatically deleted after 90 days
  • This cleanup affects all users and budgets
  • Budget data (income/expenses) is retained until you delete your account
  • You can manually clear recent activities at any time

Data Sharing

  • Your budget data is shared only with users you explicitly invite
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • Data may be shared with service providers (Firebase) for app functionality
  • We may share data if required by law

Your Rights

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your budget data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app or email us at cofinance.contact@gmail.com.

By using CoFinance, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
